- What is DMCBase?
    DMCBase is a special modification to DMCNet that allows customisation of the environment to take place. DEN files can interface with the environment in a much more open way. DMCBase is incompatible with the regular DMCNet and other modifications for DMCNet.
    
- What does it allow you to do?
    DMCBase allows the developer of a program using it to customise the environment.
